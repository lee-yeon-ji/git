Various installation methods exist by dishwasher type and kitchen design and layout.
Consult this guide to find the most suitable installation method for your dishwasher.

One key factor that influences the installation method is the cabinet baseboard, also known as the toe kick.

The term cabinet baseboard refers to the detachable plate that links the lower part of a base kitchen cabinet to the floor.
The plate is made from the same material as the kitchen cabinet, providing a seamless look to the furniture. A convertible dishwasher (which offers the possibility of freestanding or built-in installation) can be installed irrespective of the height of the cabinet baseboard.
However, it may be necessary to remove the baseboard.
When adjusting the height of the dishwasher for installation (up to 60mm for built-in only models, up to 30mm for convertible models), the allowed height of the baseboard changes according to the adjusted product height.

Installation of a built-in only model

  • Wide baseboard (150mm)

If the height of the baseboard exceeds 120mm, installation of the built-in dishwasher for the wide baseboard is recommended.

Depth of the cabinet (mm) Points to note
Greater than 600mm Standard depth, also the recommended depth, for installation.
567~600mm Installation is possible but install with care to avoid compressing the drain hose/water supply hose.
532~567mm Check beforehand as it may result in protrusion of the unit front.

Points to note
Width of the cabinet (mm) Points to note
Less than 589mm Installation is not possible. (The width is smaller than the size of the unit.)
598~600mm Installation is possible with care.
If force is used due to lack of space, physical damage to the exterior may be caused.
600~605mm Installation is possible.
Greater than 605mm Installation is not possible. (A gap could be seen on the exterior.)

Height of the cabinet (mm) Points to note
Height of the cabinet (mm) Points to note
850~880mm Up to 880mm in height is possible if a top plate is included.
This measure includes the top plate and leg adjustment. (Built-in only models are excluded.)
820~850mm Up to 850mm in height is possible if a top plate is NOT included.
This measure includes leg adjustment.
815~820mm Installation is possible.

Move left or right to check the contents.

*Top plate: The plate at the very top of the unit.
It can be attached or detached depending on the height of the cabinet.

*Leg : The unit is equipped with screw-type legs at its base, which are utilized to adjust the unit's height.

※ Caution: Install the unit in such a way that the rear of the unit is not exposed.
Fingers may get trapped behind the unit and injuries may be caused.
