At a Glance

Note
  • Replace using only LG Brand filters that are not expired. Avoid using non-LG Brand water filters and using a water filter for more than recommended period (6 months)
  • LG recommends replacing the water filter every six months. If you have been using the filter for more than 6 months, issues caused by this are not covered under Warranty
  • Use of consumable products that are NOT Authorized by LG is not covered under Warranty

  2. How to change LT500 water filter.

    1 Turn the water valve OFF that connects to the refrigerator water line and dispense any remaining water left in the water line.

    2 Remove the top shelf and move it to the lowest level.

    3 Remove the lamp cover by pressing the tab under the cover and pulling out toward the front.

    4 Remove the old filter by turning it to the left until the unlocked symbol is lined up with the indicator line and pull it down.

    5 Take the new filter out of its packaging and remove the protective cap that covers the O-rings.

    • Line up the ears so that it can be inserted into the filter head.
    • Twist the cartridge to the right to lock it into place.
    • You will feel a stop and the lock symbol should lined up with the indicator line.


    6 Turn the water supply back ON.

    • Dispense 2.5 gallons of water (flush for approximately 5 minutes).
    • Push and hold the Water Filter button on the control panel for 3 seconds to complete the process.


    Note

    You will hear a beep when you first press the button. Press and hold it for three seconds until a second beep is heard.

    7 If the water filter light remains lit, or if you need additional help, please visit our Contact Us page for further assistance

    [LG Refrigerators] How To Replace Water Filter

    Welcome to the LG customer support channel. Your LG French door refrigerator comes equipped with a water filtration system which provides you with clean fresh tasting water. Today we will show you how to properly replace the water filter on your LG French door refrigerator.

    Before you start, we recommend that you replace your water filter about every six months. Your LG French door refrigerator will indicate when the filter needs to be replaced. Additionally, you might want to replace your water filter if the water pressure from your water dispenser noticeably decreases in pressure. To purchase replacement water filter cartridges, visit your local appliance dealer or parts distributor. If located in the USA, you can also call 1-800-243-0000. If located in Canada, please call 1-888-LG Canada. Both numbers are available 24 hours a day 7 days a week. When ordering have on hand the water filter part number, ADQ36006101. This part number can be found on the water filter as well as in your owner's manual.

    To replace your water filter, follow these steps: Open the refrigerator doors. The filter housing is located on the far back left of the inside cabinet. Remove any obstacles that impede the water filter housing's full range of motion; this may include the top left shelf. Press the push button on the filter housing and pull downward. Remove the old water filter by pulling all the way downward, and then gently pull towards you until it releases and pops out. At this point there may be a small amount of water drainage. To catch any waste, use a paper towel or cup.

    When you first receive your LG French door refrigerator, you must install the water filter located in the package. When opening the filter housing for the first time, there will be a filter bypass plug used in place of the filter. Keep this plug as you might need it if a replacement filter is unavailable. The ice maker will not operate without a plug or filter in the filter compartment.

    Open the new water filter by removing the plastic protective coating to install the filter into the filter housing. Align the tabs on the side of the water filter with the housing and push in. This helps by tilting the water filter down when aligning and pushing in. When popped in, lift the water filter all the way up close the filter lid by lifting up until you hear a click.

    After replacing the filter, we recommend you flush about two and a half gallons or approximately five minutes of dispensing to remove any trapped air or contaminants from the system. Don't dispense the entire two and a half gallon amount continuously. Doing so may overload the system. Instead, press and release the dispenser pad for cycles of 30 seconds on and 60 seconds off.

    You are now ready to enjoy clean and fresh tasting water provided by your LG refrigerator.

    Thanks for watching the LG customer support channel we hope you enjoy your LG experience.

    [LG Refrigerators] How To Change The Water Filters - LPXS30886D

    Here are some indicators to look out for when it is time to change the water filters in your LG L P X S 3 0 8 8 6 D refrigerator. When the replace water filter message appears on the LCD display of your control panel, the water dispenser output decreases, or when the ice cubes are smaller than normal. LG recommends that the water filters be changed approximately every 6 months. Purchase and use replacement cartridges with the corresponding model number used on each cartridge, which can be found online or at your local home improvement store that carry LG genuine parts. Turn the water valve off that connects to your refrigerator, this sends any remaining water remove the big fresh zone drawer. Remove the three tier water filter cover. Remove the old filters by turning them counterclockwise. Remove the caps of the new water filters. Insert the new water filters into their corresponding numbered slots. Turn each filter clockwise until they are securely fastened. Reinstall the three tier water filter cover. Reinsert the big fresh zone drawer. Now turn on the water valve that connects to your refrigerator. On the control panel, select the cold water option on the LCD display window. Dispense about two and a half gallons of water to purge and prime the new water filters. Reset the replace water filter indicator message shown here on your LCD display window by holding down the water filter button for 3 seconds.
    Note

    The 3-stage filter system is only available on the LPXS30886 model at this time. Additional models may incorporate this option at a later date.

    1 Disassemble the filter cover. The filter is mounted on the left side of the fresh zone in the refrigerator.

    • Remove the left side fresh zone drawer.

    • Push, then pull out the bottom side of the filter cover to remove it.

    2 Turn the handle on the filter you wish to replace to the left by 90 degrees. Pull forward on the handle to remove the filter.

    Note

    Some water may come out of the filter during removal. Before removing the filter, place a towel in front of the case to catch the water.

    3 Remove the cap from the new filter. Hold the filter handle vertically and insert the filter. Insert the filter completely and turn the handle to the right 90 degrees.

    Note

    Before replacing the filter, ensure to remove the cap from the new filter.

    4 Remove trapped air by dispensing 2.5 gallons of water (flush for approximately 5 minutes).
    Push and hold the Water Filter button on the control panel for 3 seconds to complete the process.

    Note

    You will hear a beep when you first press the button. Press and hold it for three seconds until a second beep is heard.

    5 If the water filter light remains lit, or if you need additional help, please visit our Contact Us page for further assistance.
